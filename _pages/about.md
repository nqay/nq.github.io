I am PhD candidate in Food and Nutrition Policy and Programs at the [Friedman School of Nutrition Science and Policy, Tufts University](https://nutrition.tufts.edu/) where I am part of the [Childhood Obesity 180](https://childobesity180.tufts.edu/) lab. I earned my masters in Global Affairs (Business and Economics) from Tshingua University and B.A. in Economics and Global Health from Middlebury College. 

My research interest and areas are 
* **Child and Adolescent Nutrition and Health**
* **Women's Health and Nutrition**
* **Food Policies (safety nets, prices, taxes)**
* **Climate and Environment**
* **Metrics and Measurements (dietary, health)**
* **Digital Health**
